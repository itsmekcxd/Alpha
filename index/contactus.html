About Us

We are a group of Engineers enthusiastic in creating quality free tools and content on the internet. The main purpose of this website is to provide a comrehensive collection of free online calculators for the ease of students use and to help check their answers by themself as they are attending online classes.

The calculations on this site were grouped into 6 subjects:Transmission waveguide lines,Digital Enterprenurship,Analog Communication,Control Systems,Digital image processing and Measuring Instruments and sensors until jan 2021. All the calculators were developed in-house . Some calculators use open -source Javascript components under different open-source licenses. All the Calculators are based on well-known formulas or equations from textbooks.More than 95% of the descriptive content was developed in-house with a small amount of content taken from different text books respective to the subjects.
